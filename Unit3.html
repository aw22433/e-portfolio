Unit 3: UML

Learning outcomes

  • Be exposed to the common syntax used by the Unified Modelling Language.
  • Explore the major UML models which are used throughout the object oriented analysis, design and development process.
  • Design UML models to represent aspects of specific software systems.

    • Reflective practice

    This week we covered UML diagrams in further detail. It was also an opportunity to get hands on with some of these diagrams with the exercise to create a State machine diagram for a washing machine. While I have a deeper understanding of the different types of UML diagram, I still find some differences between them a little confusing. I believe I have a good grasp of the use case, state machine and sequence diagrams. The class diagrams, which I believe will be the most useful to me is still quite challenging to produce. While I have a stronger understanding of classes and objects, the real life application of the interactions between them is still challenging to me.
